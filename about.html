Our history.

One day, the director of the academy was approached by a film crew from America who were filming a film in Japan. They urgently needed a makeup artist who spoke English. Without hesitation, the director offered them Shu Uemura. So his career began.

Of course the choice fell on America! Hollywood, filming, professional make-up artists - all this fascinated and delighted everyone who came to study and work in this area.

So when he got on one of the sets as a make-up assistant, he was amazed at possibilities he had never seen in Japan.

And if not for one incident that changed his whole life, then who knows how many years later we would have heard about the

talented Shu Uemura. After that fame and success came to him, so much so that he managed to work with Frank Sinatra himself! Frank was so impressed with Shu Uemura's work that at the end of filming he gave him a make-up case with the caption “Shu Shu Baby”, and later the song “Shoo Shoo Baby” appeared, which reminded Uemura of their collaboration on the set.

Shu Uemura worked in Hollywood for a long time and noticed that it was very difficult for actors to wash off makeup. Each time he saw how they had to wash off and rinse off the makeup several times until the face was completely clean. This story makes you believe in chance and work on yourself, manage your goals and strive for excellence. And then everything will work out!
